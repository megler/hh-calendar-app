Who We Are...

Houndhaven, Inc. rescues dogs and puppies from euthanasia at kill shelters. We care for them until they can be placed in loving homes or with another rescue group. We believe that these lives are precious, and we are strictly a no kill organization. Our mission is life.

What

Our calendar contest gives you a chance to put your pup on display and possibly win bragging rights to your very own month in the calendar! Enter Your Dog Now!

Why

Our dogs come from high kill shelters throughout Lake and Sumter counties. New dogs are quarantined for 10 days while we monitor for health issues such as kennel cough, which is highly contagious to other dogs. During this time, we also take the dog to the vet for a physical and any necessary vaccinations. Many dogs come to us with extreme medical issues. They may have been hit by a car and have broken bones, they could be severely malnourished, they could have cancer or other issues. We will go to extreme lengths to medically save a dog, which is why fundraisers like this calendar contest are so vitally important.
